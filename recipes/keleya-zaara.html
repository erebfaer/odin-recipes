Keleya Zaara

Description

Tunisian lamb with saffron is hearty, fragrant, and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbs Olive oil
  • 750g Lamb
  • 1 1/2 tsp Saffron
  • 1 Large chopped onion
  • 25ml Water
  • 30g Parsley
  • 1 tbs Butter
  • 1 Lemon

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the lamb and cook until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
  3. Season with saffron, salt and pepper to taste; stir in all but 4 tablespoons of the onion, and pour in the water.
  4. Bring to boil, then cover, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until the lame is tender, about 15 minutes.
  5. Uncover the pan, stir in the butter, and allow the sauce to reduce for 5 to 10 minutes until it reaches the desired consistency.
  6. Season to taste with alt and pepper, then pour into a serving dish.
  7. Garnish with lemon wedges to serve.
