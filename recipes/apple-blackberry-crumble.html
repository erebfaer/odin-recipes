Apple & Blackberry Crumble

Description

A combination of sweet and tart flavours, with a crunchy top. Can be served with ice cream, custard, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 120g Plain flour
  • 60g Caster sugar
  • 90g Butter
  • 300g Cooking apples
  • 30g Demerara sugar
  • 120g Blackberries
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 190C.
  2. Mix flour and caster sugar in a large bowl.
  3. Add 60g of butter and rumb into the mixture with fingertips to make a light breadcrumb texture. Be carfeul not to overwork it.
  4. Sprinkle the mixture evenly over a baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes or until lightly coloured.
  5. Meanwhile peel, core, and dice the apples.
  6. Put the last of the butter and demerara sugar in a medium saucepan and melt together over a medium heat.
  7. Cook for 3 minutes until the mixture turns to a light caramel.
  8. Stir in the apples and cook for 3 minutes.
  9. Add the blackberries and cinnamon, and coof for 3 more minutes.
  10. Cover, remove from heat, and leave for 2 to 3 minutes to continue cooking.
  11. To serve, spoon the warm fruit into an ovenproof gratin dish, top with the cumble mix, and heat in the oven for 5 to 10 minutes.
